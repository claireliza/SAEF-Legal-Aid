Here is some information about child support and our referrals. Scroll down to start the child support questionnaire.

THE LEGAL PARENT

Only the legal parent of a child can be legally ordered to pay or receive child support. There are a few different ways one can become the legal parent of a child.

TWO TYPES OF CHILD SUPPORT ORDERS

A judicial child support order is made by a judge in court; an administrative order is made by the Department of Healthcare and Family Services. If you have a copy of your child support order, it's easy to find out which one it is.
